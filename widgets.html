How I Build

My projects usually start with curiosity, then become structure.

Even when a project begins as a feeling or a visual idea, I like shaping it into something that is easier to understand, share, and improve.

1. Notice

Start with a question.

I usually begin with an idea that feels interesting, unresolved, or worth exploring more deeply.

2. Shape

Give the idea a clear form.

That can look like a visual concept, a written piece, a sculpture, or a project layout for a website.

3. Share

Turn it into something viewable.

I want the final result to feel approachable for other people, especially across both desktop and phone screens.
